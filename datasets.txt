## 1. Celebrity Dataset
- *Source:* [Kaggle - Celeb FBI Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/pronaydebnath1/celeb-fbi)
- *Description:* A dataset containing celebrity facial images with metadata for face recognition and identification tasks.
- *Usage in Project:* Used for training/testing facial recognition and style analysis modules.

---

## 2. Myntra Product Dataset
- *Source:* [Kaggle - Myntra Products Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ronakbokaria/myntra-products-dataset)
- *Description:* A dataset containing fashion product listings from Myntra with attributes such as category, price, and brand.
- *Usage in Project:* Used for product recommendation, fashion tagging, and style matching.

---

## 3. Purchase History Dataset
- *Source:* [Kaggle - Customer Transaction Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/archit9406/customer-transaction-dataset)
- *Description:* A dataset containing anonymized customer purchase and transaction history data.
- *Usage in Project:* Used to simulate user purchase patterns and personalize fashion recommendations.
